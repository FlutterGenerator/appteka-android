<p><i>Appteka</i> is an alternative store for Android. It also can extracts APKs, are installed on your Android device and cope them into your SD card.</p><p><b>Features:</b></p><ul><li>Fast and easy to use.</li><li>Extracts almost all application, including system applications.</li><li>No ROOT access required.</li><li>Upload Apks to special Appteka.Store.</li><li>Provided Search option to search applications.</li><li>Support features of Android 7.0</li><li>Can backup and install Apks from your SD Card.</li><li>Install free apps from Appteka.Store.</li><li>Discuss apps and games with other Appteka users in realtime chat!</li></ul><p><b>Important note:</b> Appteka store is rather an „app exchange“. Users can upload apps freely, and content is only <a href='https://github.com/solkin/appteka-android/issues/4#issuecomment-594706314' target='_blank' rel='nofollow'>moderated to a degree</a>. It's your own responsibility to take proper safety measures.</p>
